ABOUT US

Renovative Design is an artificial intelligence-based architectural platform. Its main objective is to provide an effective and reliable platform that aids Canadian businesses and architects in improving the process of building design and construction, resulting in greater productivity and decreased costs. This platform helps architects, engineers, and other construction industry professionals improve both their projects and their processes by utilizing cutting-edge technologies and data-driven architectural models. They automate the generation of plans and assure compliance to Canadian-specific building regulations and codes by utilizing artificial intelligence. This framework is specifically designed for architectural firms across Canada and aims to expand these regulations and standards for other countries in the future.
